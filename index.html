I am a sessional lecturer at the University of the Witwatersrand, Johannesburg, South Africa. I was previously a MSc student at the same institution under the supervision of Prof. Benjamin Rosman and Dr. Ritesh Ajoodha. I am also an active member of the Robotics, Autonomous Intelligence and Learning Lab.

My research interests are mainly in the field of assisted reinforcement learning, including interactive reinforcement learning and human-agent interaction. I am also interested more generally in reinforcement learning and ethical and safe artificial intelligence.
